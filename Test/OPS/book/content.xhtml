Chapter 1 Title

Xu Ya’s eyes flashed for an instant, then dimmed just as quickly.

She politely addressed Master Xu as “Shizun,” but the title was pure flattery; in truth, she had spent years merely coasting under his name.

It wasn’t really her fault. Although Master Xu had spoken up to keep her inside Xianze, he had never announced to the sect that she was his disciple. Even if word had slipped out, no formal ceremony had ever been held. As a result, Xu Ya lived a shadow life, her status unrecognized, disdained by everyone.

While her mind raced, she hesitated half a second, then agreed.

Her memories had awakened. Even after amnesia and years in the cultivation world, she still worried that her boundless Fried Chicken arts might get her branded a freak and roasted on the spot.
